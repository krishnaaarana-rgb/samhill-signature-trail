A New Challenge

By Sam Hill

A Champion's Perspective on Trail Design

After more than two decades competing at the highest levels of mountain biking — from Downhill World Championships to Enduro World Series victories — I've had the privilege of riding trails across every continent. That experience hasn't just shaped me as a racer; it's given me an intimate understanding of what makes a trail truly exceptional.

Every trail tells a story. The best ones flow naturally with the terrain, challenge riders at every level, and leave you wanting to ride them again. That's the standard I'm bringing to trail design — not just technical proficiency, but the instinct that comes from thousands of runs on the world's most demanding courses.

Collaboration with Woodland Trails and Landscapes

I'm proud to partner with Woodland Trails and Landscapes for the construction side of our projects. Their expertise in sustainable trail building and land management perfectly complements our design philosophy. Together, we deliver trails that are not only thrilling to ride but built to last and respectful of the natural environment.

Comprehensive Trail Development Services

Sam Hill Signature Trail offers a complete suite of trail development services. From initial consultancy and feasibility studies through to detailed design documentation and construction supervision, we guide projects from concept to completion. Whether it's a single signature trail or a complete network, every project receives the same championship-level attention to detail.

Understanding the Art of Trail Networks

A great trail network is more than a collection of individual trails. It's an ecosystem that caters to every rider — from families experiencing mountain biking for the first time to seasoned racers pushing their limits. Our designs consider progression, flow between trails, environmental impact, and the overall rider journey.

Built for The Rider, Connected to Nature

Every trail we design starts with two questions: How will this feel to ride? And how does it work with the landscape? The best trails don't fight the terrain — they dance with it. Our approach honours the natural contours of the land while creating experiences that keep riders coming back.

This is a new chapter, but it's built on everything I've learned across 20+ years on the world's biggest stages. I'm excited to bring that experience to communities, bike parks, and trail networks worldwide.
