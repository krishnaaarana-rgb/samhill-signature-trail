What We Do

Our Services

World-class trail design and construction, backed by 20+ years of championship racing experience.

Our Approach

Championship Experience,
Applied to Every Trail

Sam Hill collaborates with local governments, mountain bike clubs, bike parks, and trail-building companies worldwide. Every project benefits from Sam's unique perspective as a 5x World Champion who has ridden and raced on trails across 30+ countries.

This unparalleled experience translates directly into trail design that prioritises flow, safety, and rider experience. Whether it's a community trail network or a world-class bike park, Sam brings the same elite-level precision to every engagement.

Partnered with Woodland Trails and Landscapes, we deliver end-to-end solutions from initial consultancy through to construction supervision and final sign-off.

Services in Detail

What We Deliver

01

Consultancy

Expert on-site consultancy and strategic planning to help you make informed decisions about your trail infrastructure investment. Sam provides firsthand knowledge of what makes trails ride well at every level.

  • Feasibility studies for new trail networks
  • Advice for councils, developers, and bike park operators
  • Site assessments and terrain analysis
  • Stakeholder engagement and community consultation
  • Strategic planning for trail tourism destinations
02

Trail Concepts & Detailed Design

From initial trail concepts to construction-ready documentation. Every trail is designed with the flow, technicality, and precision that only a World Champion's perspective can deliver.

  • From initial trail concepts to construction-ready documentation
  • Trail alignments mapped with race-proven flow principles
  • Drainage design and environmental considerations
  • Feature specifications for jumps, berms, rock gardens
  • Rider experience optimisation across all skill levels
03

Park Design

Complete mountain bike park planning that creates integrated riding destinations. Sam designs parks that cater to every rider, from first-timers to seasoned racers, with progressive trail networks that keep people coming back.

  • Complete mountain bike park planning
  • Trail network design with progressive difficulty ratings
  • Skills areas and pump tracks
  • Jump lines and flow trails
  • Supporting facilities and infrastructure planning
04

Construction Supervision

Partnered with Woodland Trails and Landscapes, we provide on-site oversight ensuring every trail is built to championship standard. Our construction supervision guarantees the design intent is faithfully translated into the finished product.

  • Partnered with Woodland Trails and Landscapes
  • On-site oversight ensuring championship-standard build quality
  • Quality control and specification compliance
  • Sustainable construction methods
  • Post-build assessment and sign-off
