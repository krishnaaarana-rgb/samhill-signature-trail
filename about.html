About Us

A Legacy Built On The Trail

From 5x World Championship titles to shaping the world's most thrilling mountain bike trails.

The Rider

Sam Hill

Sam Hill is one of the most decorated mountain bikers in history — a 5x World Champion in Enduro and Downhill with over 20 years competing at the highest level across 30+ countries. His career has taken him to the most demanding courses on every continent, giving him an intimate understanding of what makes a trail truly exceptional.

Now, Sam is channelling that unparalleled experience into trail design and construction. Every trail is built with the precision of a racer's eye — understanding how riders read terrain, where flow matters most, and what transforms a good trail into an unforgettable one.
